# LeadSens Product Positioning

## Value Proposition

**Primary:** "Your outbound on autopilot — using your own tools"

**Supporting:**
1. **Speed:** From ICP to running campaign in one conversation
2. **Intelligence:** 3D scoring (fit + intent + timing) saves 40% on enrichment
3. **Quality:** 6-step email frameworks with quality gates, not generic templates
4. **Control:** BYOT — use Instantly, Smartlead, Apollo, HubSpot you already pay for
5. **Automation:** A/B testing, reply management, CRM routing — all automated

## Workflow Steps (for How It Works)

1. **Connect your tools** — Link ESP, CRM, enrichment tools
2. **Describe your ICP** — Natural language → precise filters (99.8% accuracy)
3. **AI handles the pipeline** — Source → Score → Enrich → Draft → Send
4. **Monitor & optimize** — Real-time analytics, auto A/B, reply management

## Target Persona

**Primary:** Solo founders and small sales teams (2-10 people) doing outbound B2B

**Pain points:**
- Too many tools, none of them talk to each other
- Hours wasted on manual lead research and enrichment
- Generic email templates get ignored
- No time to A/B test or analyze results
- Paying for multiple tools but using 20% of features

**Dream outcome:** "I describe my ideal customer and wake up to meetings booked"
